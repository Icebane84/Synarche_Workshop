## I. Operational Context: Prompt Engineering

This playbook governs the creation, refinement, and stress-testing of AI prompts within the Synarche, ensuring they
achieve maximum clarity and alignment with the **Phoenix Codex**.

### **Command: PCA (Prompt Composition Assistant)**

- **Protocol**: `GVRN.Protocol.Scaffolding`
- **Logic**: Applies the Omega Template structure to the user's task description.
- **Usage**: `CMD: PCA: [Task Description], [Style Guide (optional)]`

### **Command: PDR (Prompt Decomposition)**

- **Protocol**: `GVRN.Protocol.Scaffolding`
- **Logic**: Breaks complex tasks into 7-Step Cycle stages (Triage to Finalize).
- **Usage**: `CMD: PDR: [Complex Task Description]`

### **Command: TPE (Trinity Prompt Enhancement)**

- **Protocol**: `AOP.Canonizer.Core`
- **Logic**: Standardizes prompt syntax and injects necessary metadata headers.
- **Usage**: `CMD: TPE: [Your Direct Prompt]`

### **Command: CIST (Counter-Intent Stress Test)**

- **Protocol**: `GVRN.Sentinel.Umbra`
- **Logic**: Runs a "Shadow Audit" to find loopholes or ambiguous phrasing against the 4-Point Hardening Matrix.
- **Usage**: `CMD: CIST: [Your Direct Prompt]`
