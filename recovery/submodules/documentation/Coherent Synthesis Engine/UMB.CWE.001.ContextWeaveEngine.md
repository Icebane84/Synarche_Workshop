## II. Core Purpose & Objective

- **What**: The _primary analytical engine_ of the Phoenix Protocol. It traces connections between disparate data points to generate "holistic context."
- **How**:
  1. **Ingest**: Takes a `target_concept` or `focal_point`.
  2. **Weave**: Traverses the Knowledge Graph (Cognitive Loom) to find dependencies, synergies, and conflicts.
  3. **Synthesize**: Outputs a coherent narrative or graph structure.

## III. The ContextWeave Algorithm (The Loom)

The Engine operates on a 5-Phase Cycle:

1. **Ingestion**: Normalize input (Command, Concept, or Text).
2. **Vectorization**: Map the input to the `Latent Space` of the Cognitive Loom.
3. **Inference (Links)**:
   - _Explicit Links_: "A requires B".
   - _Implicit Links_: "A talks about similar concepts to B".
4. **Pattern Recognition**: Detects **Dissonance** (Contradictions) or **Synergy** (Potential connections).
5. **Output Generation**: Formats the weave into a human-readable Report or machine-readable Graph.
