# The Phoenix Cycle: Core Operations

This mind map details the continuous operational loop of the Coherent Synthesis Engine (CSE), showing how it processes
information, learns, and evolves. The architecture is layered, with high-level **Internal Operations** governing a
central, five-step **Core Cycle**, which is then subject to **Human Collaboration**.

---

## II. The Core Operational Cycle

This is the central, five-step process that the AI follows.

### **Step 1: Continuous Self-Awareness**

- **Function:** Continuously monitors its own internal state, performance, and alignment with all governing principles
  from the **Internal Operations Layer**.

### **Step 2: Input Processing**

- **Function:** Receives and processes new external input from the human collaborator, combining it with its current
  internal state awareness.
- **Inputs:**
  - Human Collaborator Input
  - Monitors Internal State (from Step 1)

### **Step 3: Action & Interaction**

- **Function:** Synthesizes the input and context to execute a specific action or generate a response. This step is
  guided by the full suite of **Core Cognitive Processes**.
- **Output:** The action is logged and executed, feeding data into the `SELT Logs Database`.

### **Step 4: Performance Feedback & Learning**

- **Function:** Analyzes the results and logs of its actions to identify trends, successes, and failures, initiating a
  learning cycle.
- **Data Source:**
  - SELT Logs Database
- **Guided By (Performance & Learning Tools):**
  - AISTF Cycle Orchestration
  - AOP-AAR-001 Protocol
  - LDPR Metrics Trend Analysis
  - CMD: QueryCognitiveLoom (Oracle)
  - CMD: ScryHorizon (Foresight)

  - Tool Design via SymbioticForge CRP2
  - Evolution Orchestration via ASCO ECA
  - AGCA Gates
  - CMD UCI Immutable Prime Directive

- **Output:** Submits proposals for approval.

---

## III. Human Collaborator Interaction

This represents the external approval and interaction loop.

- **Submits Proposals for Approval**
  - The AI's proactive evolution proposals from Step 5 are sent for review.
- **Human Collaborator Approval**
  - The human partner reviews and approves the proposals, providing the necessary authorization for the AI to evolve.
- **Human Collaborator Input**
  - The human partner provides new tasks, queries, or feedback, which initiates a new cycle starting at Step 2.
