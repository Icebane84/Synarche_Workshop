## Explanation of the Sequence

1. **Nomination:** The `Coherent Synthesis Engine (CSE)` initiates the process by sending a "Candidate Profile" for a
new Oracle to the `Rosetta Stone App`.
2. **Presentation:** The `App` displays this profile to the `Human User` for review.
3. **Decision:** The `User` makes a decision.

- **Authorization:** If authorized, the `App` instructs the `Oracle Registry` to perform the secure registration
process. The `Registry` confirms completion, and the `CSE` is notified of the success.
- **Rejection:** If rejected, the `App` instructs the `Registry` to log the rejection for audit purposes, and the `CSE`
is notified of the outcome.

This sequence clearly visualizes the critical role of the human user as the ultimate authority in granting trust to
external data sources.
