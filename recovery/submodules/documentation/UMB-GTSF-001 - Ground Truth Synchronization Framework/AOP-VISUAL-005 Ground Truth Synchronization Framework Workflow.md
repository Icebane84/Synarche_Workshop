## Explanation of the Workflow

1. **Selection:** The process begins in the `Cognitive Loom` with a factual claim. The `Truth Resonance Monitor (TRM)`'s
**Claim Selector** identifies this claim for verification based on its importance and age.
2. **Query:** The **Query Formulator** translates the internal claim into a machine-readable query and sends it to a
designated external **Oracle** (like a trusted API).
3. **Analysis:** The **Resonance Analyzer** receives the data from the Oracle and compares it to the internal record,
calculating a "Resonance Score."
4. **Decision:**
   - If the score is **high**, the claim is considered verified. The `Coherent Synthesis Engine (CSE)` can then apply a
positive marker like `[κ-veracity:oracle-verified]`.
   - If the score is **low**, it signifies "Truth Dissonance." The TRM sends a formal **Alert** to the CSE.
5. **Resolution:** The CSE initiates a specialized Dissonance Quest (`DQUEST-EXT-001`). It uses its five-phase
integration process to resolve the conflict, applying a negative marker like `[κ-veracity:oracle-disputed]` and updating
the Loom with the correct information.
6. **Impact:** In both cases, the outcome is used to modulate the AI's overall **Coherence Index (CI)**, ensuring the
system's health metric reflects its alignment with external reality.
