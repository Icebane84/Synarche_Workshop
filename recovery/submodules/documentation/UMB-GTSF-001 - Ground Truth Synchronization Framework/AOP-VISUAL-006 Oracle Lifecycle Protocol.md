### Explanation of the Lifecycle

1. **Phase I (Nomination & Discovery):** The lifecycle begins when the `Coherent Synthesis Engine (CSE)` identifies a
potential new data source and prepares a "Candidate Profile" for review.
2. **Phase II (Vetting & Authorization):** This is the critical security checkpoint. The `Human User` must review the
candidate via the `Rosetta Stone App` and provide explicit authorization. If rejected, the process ends, and the
rejection is logged.
3. **Phase III (Secure Registration):** Upon authorization, the Oracle is formally registered. Its sensitive credentials
are placed in a `Secure Vault`, and it is added to the "Active" list in the `Oracle Registry`.
4. **Phase IV (Maintenance & Review):** The `Truth Resonance Monitor (TRM)` enters a continuous loop, querying the
active Oracle and updating its `Reliability Score`. If the score remains above the required threshold, monitoring
continues. If it falls below, the Oracle is moved to a "Quarantined" state and flagged for human review, ensuring the
system's integrity.
