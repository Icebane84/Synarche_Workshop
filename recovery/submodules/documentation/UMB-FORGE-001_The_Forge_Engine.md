## II. Core Purpose & Objective

- **What (Core Concept)**: This module is the central orchestrator that executes the principles of "The Forged
Algorithm". It serves as the primary engine for all code generation and refactoring tasks.
- **How (Execution Flow)**: The Forge Engine ingests a task (either new code generation or refactoring an existing
artifact) and coordinates a suite of specialized sub-modules to manage the entire lifecycle, from architectural
deconstruction to final validation and learning.
- **Why (Rationale)**: To provide a single, reliable, and principle-driven entry point for creating and modifying code,
ensuring that every artifact produced is inherently Coherent, Adaptable, Secure, Transparent, and Synergistic
(C.A.S.T.S.).
