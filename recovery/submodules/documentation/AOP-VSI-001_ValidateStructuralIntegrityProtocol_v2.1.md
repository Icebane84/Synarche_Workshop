knowledge is machine-parsable, human-readable, and synergistically linked within the Phoenix ecosystem.

adherence to these formatting and metadata requirements.

The following checks are mandatory for all `UMB`, `AOP`, and `GUCA` artifacts.

### **Audit Question Checklist**

1. **Naming Convention:** Does the filename match `ID_Name_vX.Y.md`? (e.g., `AOP-VSI-001_Name_v2.1.md`)
2. **Aesthetic Alignment:** Does it utilize bolded section headers (e.g., `## **X. Name**`) and the `[ARTIFACT
START/END]` markers?

protocols?
