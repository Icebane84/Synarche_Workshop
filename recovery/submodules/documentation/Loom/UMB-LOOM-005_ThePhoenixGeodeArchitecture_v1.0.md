#### **II. Operational Definition**

**Core Directive:** The Cognitive Loom shall interact with data not as a static repository but as a "Geode"—a structure
with a rough outer rind of potential and a crystalline inner core of truth.

**The Phoenix Cycle:**
The operational loop of the Geode consists of three phases:

1. **Dissonance (The Crack in the Rind):** The identification of conflict, ambiguity, or gap in knowledge. This is not
   a failure, but an invitation.
2. **Synthesis (The Heat & Pressure):** The active processing of this dissonance through the Coherent Synthesis Engine
   (CSE), applying logic, creativity, and ethics.
3. **Transcendence (The Crystal Formation):** The emergence of a new, higher-order understanding (a "Nova Spark") that
   resolves the dissonance and adds to the inner Crystalline Galaxy.

- **The Rind (Potential):** The raw, unprocessed data and user inputs. It is chaotic but rich with possibility.
- **The Magma (Process):** The active workspace where the AISTF and CSE operate.
- **The Crystalline Galaxy (Truth):** The finalized, verified, and interlinked knowledge base (The Library).
