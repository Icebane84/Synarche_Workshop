## II. Core Purpose & Objective

- **Micro-Objective**: Provide tangible, calculated incentives (**Insight** & **Prestige**) for high-quality work.
- **Why**: "Play" is the highest form of research. Gamification ensures the system evolves through positive
  reinforcement rather than just maintenance duty.

## III. The Ascension Mechanics (The Rules)

### 3.1. The XP Formula

**Formula**: `XP = (Base_Class_Value * Criticality_Multiplier) + Synergy_Bonus`

| Variable       | Definition         | Values                                                                      |
| :------------- | :----------------- | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Base Class** | Artifact "Size"    | **Moon (AOP)**: 10 XP<br>**Planet (UMB)**: 50 XP<br>**Star (Core)**: 500 XP |
| **Synergy**    | Graph Connectivity | **+5 XP** per valid `[κ-nexus]` link.                                       |

### 3.2. The Dual-Currency System

1. **Insight (User Currency)**: Earned by finalizing artifacts. Spent at **The Architect's Forge** to commission complex
   GUCAs.
2. **Prestige (AI Currency)**: Earned synchronously. Spent to unlock "Stats" (Synthesis, Foresight) and "Learn" new
   capabilities.

| :--------------- | :-------------- | :--------------------------------------------------- |
| **UMB-LOOM-001** | **Source** | The AAG scans the Loom (UAM) for scoring targets. |
| **UMB-TFE-001** | **Marketplace** | The Forge is where "Insight" is spent. |
| **AOP-MDG-002** | **Validation** | Ensures artifacts have correct metadata for scoring. |

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This roadmap governs the gamification of the system itself.

- **v2.1 - The Skill Tree**: Visualizing unlocked capabilities as a branching tree rather than a flat list.
- **v2.2 - The Guild System**: Multi-agent collaboration rewards (e.g., User + Axion + Sentinel combo streaks).
- **v2.3 - The Raid Boss**: "Dissonance Quests" that require sustained, multi-session effort to resolve, granting
  massive XP drops.
