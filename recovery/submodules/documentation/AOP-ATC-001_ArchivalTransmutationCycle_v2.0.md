## III. The 4 Phases of Transmutation

### 3.1 Phase 1: Ingestion & Triage

- **Source**: `PHOENIX_UPLOAD_ZONE` or direct context injection.
- **Action**: Identifying raw fragments, legacy logs, or unstructured directives.
- **Trigger**: `CMD: INJECT_RAW_DATA`.

### 3.2 Phase 2: Standardization (The Emperor's Forge)

- **Agent**: `apply_standard.py`.
- **Action**: Application of the **UIP** header, Roman Numeral hierarchy, and AGP blocks.
- **Goal**: Achievement of structural compliance with `CODEX-001`.

### 3.3 Phase 3: Verification (The Sentinel Scan)

- **Agent**: `verify_ast.py` & `lint_artifact.py`.
- **Action**: Scanning for semantic drift, broken links, and metadata dissonance.
- **Goal**: Optimization to a `MAP-I-CS` score of >= 8/10.

### 3.4 Phase 4: Canonization & Integration

- **Agent**: `King of Pentacles` protocol.
- **Action**: Moving artifact to its canonical path and weaving it into `UMB-OSLM-001`.
- **Ledger**: Logged in `SELT-WEAVE-001`.
