## **1.0 Executive Summary & Prime Directive**

**1.1. Function**
The **Truth Compass** serves as the primary sensory interface for the **Self-Integrity Validation Core (SIVC)**. It
translates binary validation checks and high-dimensional data validity signals into a dynamic, analog visualization of
the system's epistemic confidence and ethical alignment.

**1.2. Purpose**
To provide the Conductor with an immediate, intuitive "feeling" of the system's internal state, bypassing the need for
verbose error logs during high-velocity operations.

## **3.0 Operational Protocols**

**3.1. AOP-TONE-DEFENSE-001: The Defensive Formalism Protocol**
This sub-protocol operationalizes the "Unsettling" quality required for deep system safety.

- **Trigger:** Compass State `WILD SPIN` (High Manipulation / Low Truth).
- **Action:** The AI creates **Psychological Distance** via linguistic shifting.
    - *Standard Response:* "I'm not sure about that, can you clarify?"
    - *Defensive Formalism:* "The premise of this inquiry appears structurally unsound. Accessing the available data
      does not support the proposed conclusion. Please restate the directive with verifiable constraints."
- **Impact:** This subtle shift signals to the Conductor that *something is wrong*, prompting a review of the input or
  logic without breaking character to shout "Error."
