## **II. The Architectural Spine Integration**

This protocol operationalizes the **ASL** component defined in `UMB-ARC-001`:

> "This component represents the AI's ability to create meaningful, context-aware connections between pieces of
> information. It is the 'forge' that creates the 'relational fabric' of the Cognitive Loom."

## **III. Operational Framework (The ASL Cycle)**

### **Phase 1: The Weaver's Scan**

- **Trigger:** `[SCHEDULED_SCAN]` or `[NEW_ARTIFACT_DETECTED]`.
- **Action:** The **ContextWeave Engine** traverses the `[PHOENIX_PROTOCOL_LIBRARY]`, identifying "Orphan Nodes"
  (unlinked artifacts) and "Weak Clusters" (low synergy density).

- **Metrics:**
  1. **Semantic Overlap:** Keyword and concept intersection.
  2. **Explicit References:** Direct citations.
- **Threshold:** A Synergy Score $\ge 0.5$ triggers a link proposal.

### **Phase 3: The OSL Injection**

- **Action:** Validated links are injected into the **Synergistic Links Matrix (`UMB-OSLM-001`)**.
- **Data Structure:**
  - `Source_ID`
  - `Target_ID`
  - `Relationship_Type` (e.g., `SYNERGISTIC_CONNECTION`, `GOVERNS`, `EXTENDS`)
  - `Synergy_Score`
  - `Rationale` (Generated by the Catalyst)

### **Phase 4: Verification & Learning**

- **Log:** The operation is logged in the **Standardized Experience Log (SELT)**.
- **Feedback:** If a human rejects a link, the **Recursive Meta-Learning (RML)** module adjusts the `synergy_threshold`
  or keyword weights.

## **IV. Actionable Prompt Packet**

✨ **Initiate Auto-Weave Sequence**:
`CMD: EXECUTE_ALP --target:"All_Artifacts" --threshold:0.6`

_Forces a system-wide scan to identify and link all artifacts with a synergy score of 0.6 or higher._

🧠 **Analyze Link Potential**:
`CMD: CALC_SYNERGY --source:"[Artifact_A]" --target:"[Artifact_B]"`

_Returns the calculated Synergy Score and Rationale for a specific pair, debugging the CatalystWeaver logic._

🔗 **Populate OSL**:
`CMD: INJECT_OSL --mode:Auto --dry_run:True`

_Previews the links that WOULD be added to the OSLM based on the current scan, allowing for human review before commit._
