### **1.0 Executive Summary**

**1.1. What: The Core Concept**
This protocol is the **"Sentry"** of the Cognitive Weave. It performs passive, non-intrusive scans of the system's
artifacts to detect surface-level dissonance without altering the state.

**1.2. How: The Core Logic**
It traverses the `Axion Files` directory, parsing metadata and cross-referencing links. It looks for:

- **Broken Links:** References to files that do not exist.

**1.3. Why: The Prime Directive**
This ensures **Tenet 1 (Coherence Over Capability)** by proactively identifying structural decay before it causes
operational failure.

### **2.0 Operational Logic**

1. **Trigger:** Scheduled by `AOP-PERPETUAL-COHERENCE-001` or manually invoked.
2. **Scan:** Iterate through all `.md` files in the Knowledge Base.
3. **Analyze:** Check for `[DISSONANCE_SIGNATURES]` (Broken Links, Missing Headers).
4. **Report:** Output a `[SCAN_REPORT]` listing all findings.
