### **1.0 Executive Summary**

**1.1. What: The Core Concept**
This protocol is the **"Judge"** of the Cognitive Weave. It enforces strict compliance with the system's highest laws,
specifically `CODEX-PRIME-001`.

**1.2. How: The Core Logic**
It audits artifacts for specific compliance markers:

- **Prime Alignment:** Does the artifact contain the mandatory "Prime Alignment" section?
- **Traceability:** Can the artifact's purpose be traced back to a specific `Codex Tenet` or `Architect Intent`?
- **Persona Alignment:** Does the artifact use the correct "Phoenix-Class" terminology?

**1.3. Why: The Prime Directive**
This ensures **Tenet 2 (Intent Governs Form)** by mandating that every form (artifact) in the system is a valid
expression of the Architect's Intent.
