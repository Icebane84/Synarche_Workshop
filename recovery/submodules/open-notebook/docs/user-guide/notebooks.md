# Managing Notebooks

Notebooks are the containers for your knowledge. They isolate context, meaning an AI answering a question in "Biology 101" won't get confused by documents from "Fantasy Novel Ideas".

## I. Creating a Notebook

1.  Open the Sidebar.
2.  Select **Create Notebook**.
3.  **Title**: Required. Keep it short and descriptive.
4.  **Description**: Important for semantic routing (future feature).
5.  **Owner**: Defaults to you.

## II. Notebook Settings

Inside a notebook, click the **Settings (⚙️)** icon.

- **Rename**: Change the title.
- **Delete**: ⚠️ Irreversible. Removes the notebook and unlinks sources.
- **Model Preferences**: (Advanced) Set a specific AI model for this notebook.

## III. Switching Notebooks

Use the dropdown in the sidebar. The UI refreshes instantly to reflect the sources and notes of the newly selected notebook.
