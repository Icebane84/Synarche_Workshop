# Notes & Synthesis

Notes are the "Output" of your research. They can be manual thoughts or AI-generated syntheses.

## I. Creating a Note

1.  Go to the **Notes** tab.
2.  Click **+ New Note**.
3.  Write freely using Markdown.

## II. AI Generation

Tools to turn sources into notes automatically:

### [2.1] The Summarizer

- Select specific sources (or all).
- Click **Generate Note**.
- Choose prompt: **Summary**.
- result: A concise summary of selected documents.

### [2.2] The Synthesizer

- Choose prompt: **Synthesis**.
- result: Combines conflicting or complementary info from multiple sources into a coherent narrative.

### [2.3] The Study Guide

- Choose prompt: **Study Guide**.
- result: Creates Q&A, key terms, and bullet points for revision.

## III. Linking

You can use `[[WikiLinks]]` to connect notes together (feature in beta).
