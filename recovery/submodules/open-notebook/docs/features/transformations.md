# Transformations

Transformations are structured AI workflows that turn raw data into specific output formats. Unlike free-form chat, these are deterministic pipelines designed for repeatable results.

## I. Core Transformation Types

### [1.1] The Summarizer

**Input**: One or more documents.
**Process**: Recursive summarization (map-reduce) if text is long.
**Output**: A bulleted or narrative summary.

### [1.2] The Study Guide

**Input**: Lecture notes, textbooks, articles.
**Output**:

- Key Concepts Definitions
- Quiz Questions (with answers hidden or separate)
- Timeline of events (if applicable)

### [1.3] The Synthesizer

**Input**: Multiple differing viewpoints (e.g., 3 news articles on the same topic).
**Process**: Identifies common facts, conflicting reports, and unique details.
**Output**: A cohesive "State of the Union" report.

## II. Custom Transformations (Advanced)

You can define your own transformation prompts in `prompts/custom_transforms.yaml`.
_Example_: "Turn this into a Twitter Thread" or "Extract all financial figures into a CSV table."
