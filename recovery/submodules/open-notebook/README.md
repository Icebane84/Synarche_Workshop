# Open Notebook 📓

**Private, AI-Powered Research Assistant**

Open Notebook is a local-first application that helps you aggregate, analyze, and synthesize knowledge from PDFs,
websites, video transcripts, and more. It uses advanced AI to ground every answer in your specific sources.
