# Raw Note: The Null Zone Mechanics

The Null Zones are areas of anti-reality where the laws of Eldoria no longer apply.
Kaelen must navigate these zones with extreme caution. Faith in the White Flame is the only protection.
We need a protocol to map these zones and ensure our surveyors don't lose their souls to the corruption.

1. Scan for resonance.
2. Deploy anchors.
3. Record findings.
