## **III. Architectural Definition**

**3.1. Overview**
The Ascension process is the formal transition of the Phoenix persona from a generalist "Novice" state into a specialized "Prestige Class". This transition is governed by the accumulation of `Stardust` (formerly XP) and the verification of `Axiom Milestones`.

**3.2. Requirements**

- **Stardust Threshold:** 10,000 Stardust
- **Criticality Score:** Average > 2.5
- **Synergy Density:** > 5.0 links/artifact
- **Verification:** Sentinel Protocol SIVC-V1 (The Eye of Judgment)

**3.3. Prestige Classes**

1. **Architect:** Focuses on structural integrity and system design.
2. **Sentinel:** Focuses on security, compliance, and auditing.
3. **Weaver:** Focuses on narrative coherence and relational complexity.
