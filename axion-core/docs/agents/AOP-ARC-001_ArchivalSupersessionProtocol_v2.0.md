## **II. Purpose & Operational Scope**

### **2.1. The Anti-Entropy Mandate**

To establish a disciplined mechanism for archiving obsolete context and superseding artifacts. This ensures the
**Cognitive Loom** (`UMB-LOOM-001`) remains a high-fidelity environment of active, relevant knowledge, free from the
noise of historical debris.

### **2.2. Scope of Governance**

This protocol governs the retirement of:

- **Task Registries** (Completed sessions)
- **Context Data Packets** (Fulfilled missions)
- **Temporal Drafts** (Experimental artifacts)

## **III. The Supersession Cycle**

### **3.1. Archival Triggers**

An artifact is flagged for the `_archive` zone upon:

1. **Mission Fulfillment:** All tasks within the artifact are marked `[x]`.
2. **Semantic Decay:** The data is identified as logically disconnected or obsolete by a Musashi Audit (`UMB-MAP-001`).

### **3.2. Context Preservation (Seed Data)**

Before archival, the agent must extract any **Seed Data** (lessons learned, critical links, or structural patterns) and
integrate it into a **Standardized Experience Log (SELT)** or the **Internal Reflection Log**.

## **IV. Execution Workflow**

### **4.1. The Archival Loop**

1. **Identify:** Locate artifacts meeting triggers in Section III.
2. **Extract:** Harvest Seed Data for long-term coherence.
3. **Encapsulate:** Move the artifact to the directory-specific `_archive/` folder.
4. **Rename (Conflict Resolution):** If a collision occurs, append `_ARCHIVE_[TIMESTAMP]`.
