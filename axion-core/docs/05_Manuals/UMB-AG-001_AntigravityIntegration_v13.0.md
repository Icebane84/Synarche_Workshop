internally references the **Phoenix Rosetta Stone (PRS-001)** to select templates and synthesize information.

component being critiqued (e.g., "In the UMB's 'How' section..."). This systematically refines the AI's logic.

### 3.4. Pillar 4: Self-Improvement (The OGLN Nexus)

All feedback and successful artifacts are treated as new data points. They are implicitly incorporated into the OGLN's
knowledge nexus, ensuring consistency and continuous alignment with evolving standards.
