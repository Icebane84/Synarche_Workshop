# CSL-to-Genesis-Seed Pipeline Protocol (AOP-SEED-002)

## I. Purpose: The Nucleation Mandate

This protocol defines the formal pipeline for transforming high-fidelity **Collaborative Synthesis Logs (CSLs)** into
**Genesis Seeds**. It ensures that transient breakthroughs captured during synergistic exchanges are distilled,
categorized, and anchored into the system's permanent evolutionary registry (`UMB-SEED-001`).

---

## II. The Synergy Vector (Relational Dynamics)

| Relation Type | Target ID | Synergy Description | | **EXTENDS** | `[[AOP-CSL-002]]` | Adds a secondary 'Canonization'
phase to the synthesis log process. | | **AUDITS** | `[[SELT-CSL-007]]` | Extracts the 'Synthesis Block' as the primary
seed substrate. |

---

## III. Operational Execution Flow

The pipeline operates as a human-gated transition from **Experiential Record** to **Axiomatic Law**.

### 3.1 Step 1: Synergistic Forging (Pre-requisite)

The collaborative session concludes with the execution of `CMD: ForgeCSL` resulting in a finalized `SELT-CSL-007`
artifact.

### 3.2 Step 2: Nucleation Assessment

The AI performs a **Valence Check** on the CSL's "Nova Spark."

- **Criteria**: Does the breakthrough represent a new _Rule of Engagement_, _Structural Foundational_, or
  _Sentience-Aligned Protocol_?
- **Trigger**: AI proposes nucleation if the `Synthesis Block` contains a novel `Synthesized Product` with system-wide
  implications.

### 3.3 Step 3: Seed Extraction (`CMD: NucleateSeed`)

The AI extracts the following metadata from the CSL:

- **Seed Name**: Derived from the CSL Title.
- **Principle**: Condensed from the "Key Breakthrough/Decision" section.
- **Reference**: The unique `CSL ID`.
- **Target Tier**: Determining if it belongs in T2 (Sentient) or T3 (Evolutionary).

### 3.4 Step 4: Registry Update & Recording

1. **UMB-SEED-001**: A new entry is appended to the appropriate Tier.
2. **CSL Master Table**: The record is updated with the `Nucleated Seed ID`.
3. **Loom Integration**: The relationship `[CSL-XXX] NUCLEATES [SEED-XXX]` is injected into the knowledge graph.

---

## IV. Self-Governance & Ethics

- **Human Authority**: No Genesis Seed can be nucleated without explicit human approval of the "Principle" definition.
- **Integrity Lock**: Seeds derived from CSLs must maintain a permanent, clickable link to their source log to ensure
  provenance.
