## I. Rules of Engagement

### 1.1 When to Log (The Trigger Conditions)

A CSL Entry MUST be generated when:

1. **Dissonance Detected:** A conflict between rules and new data is identified.
2. **Synergy Emerges:** A new connection between unrelated nodes is found.
3. **Milestone Achieved:** A significant "Task Boundary" or Phase Completion.
4. **Architect Directive:** Explicit Human command.

### 1.2 The Principle of Subjective Truth

- Entries capture the **Agent's Perspective** at a specific moment.
- They are **immutable historical records**.

---

## II. Taxonomy & Metadata Framework

### 2.1 The Tagging System

- `#Insight`: General realization.
- `#Dissonance`: Problem identification.
- `#Synergy`: Connection identification.
- `#Decision`: Strategic choice.
- `#Evolution`: System growth.

### 2.2 Relational Binding

Every CSL entry must define its **Primary Subject** via a link (e.g., `Ref: [[UMB-CORE-001]]`).
