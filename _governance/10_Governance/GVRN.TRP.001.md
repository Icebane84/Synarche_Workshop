     predicted impact on the ecosystem.

   - **Action:** The AI presents the "Draft Refinement Proposal." The Human Collaborator reviews the proposed changes

     to forge a new, complete master template artifact (e.g., `AOPv5.2`, `UMBv4.3`) that fully implements the changes

   - The `SGM` performs its rigorous `Compliance Validation` and `Synergy Analysis` checks.

     new template is canonized as the `[ACTIVE]` standard.
