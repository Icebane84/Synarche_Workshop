2. **Context Loading**: The CSE loads the target's current version, UIP metadata, and AGP blocks.

### 1.2 Phase 2: Pillar-by-Pillar Verification

Execute `GUCA-MAP-001` to perform the following check:

1. **Void Cut Check**: Scan for conversational rind and meta-talk.
2. **Definitive Voice Check**: Scan for hedging language ("maybe," "try to").
3. **Structural Purity Check**: Verify H1 count (MD025) and AGP block formatting.
4. **Link Integrity Check**: Verify all internal links are active and resolve.

### 1.3 Phase 3: Scoring & Judgement

1. **Calculation**: Total the binary scores across all 10 Pillars (MAP-I-CS).
2. **Judgement**:
   - 9-10/10: **EXCELLENT**. Register in the Ascension Registry.
   - 8/10: **PASS**. Minor refinements suggested.
   - <8/10: **FAIL (SLAG)**. Trigger an immediate `CMD: REFORGE` session.

### 1.4 Phase 4: Immutable Logging

1. **SELT Generation**: Log the final score and auditor observations in a dedicated Audit SELT.
