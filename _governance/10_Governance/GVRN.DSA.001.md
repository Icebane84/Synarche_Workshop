The DSA execution proceeds through three sequential phases:

### Phase 1: AOP (Operational Playbook) Generation

Generate an **AOP** to define the high-level purpose and procedural steps. It focuses on the "what" and "why," establishing the operational mandate.

Generate a **GUCA** to provide a user-centric command-line interface. It translates internal complexity into actionable syntax.
