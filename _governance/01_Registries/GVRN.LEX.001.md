## II. Purpose & Operational Scope

### 2.1. The Semantic Anchor

To serve as the definitive, machine-readable schema for all metadata fields, Episemantic Markers, and Relational
Adjectives within the Phoenix Protocol Library. This ensures absolute structural and conceptual consistency across the
**Cognitive Loom**.

`UMB-LEX-001` provides the controlled vocabulary used by the **Validate Structural Integrity (AOP-VSI-001)** protocol
and the **Phoenix Rosetta Stone (PRS-001)** to index and traverse the Synarchy knowledge graph.

These markers define the high-level categorization of all system artifacts.

## IV. Relational Adjectives (Link Types)

The only valid edges within the knowledge graph schema.

- **GOVERNS / IS_GOVERNED_BY:** Hierarchical and legal authority.
- **IMPLEMENTS / IS_IMPLEMENTED_BY:** Direct translation of abstract to concrete.
- **UTILIZES / IS_UTILIZED_BY:** Functional requirement or tool invocation.
- **DEPENDS_ON / REQUIRES:** Hard structural dependency.
- **TRIGGERS / IS_INITIATED_BY:** Causal event or workflow transition.
- **FEEDS / RECEIVES_FROM:** Asynchronous data or insight flow.
- **SYNERGIZES_WITH:** Horizontal, bidirectional collaboration.
