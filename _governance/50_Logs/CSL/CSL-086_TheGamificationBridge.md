## I. The Nova Spark Event ✨

### 1.1 Context (The Gap)

The **Phoenix RPG Framework (`UMB-RPG-001`)** and the **Hephaestus Engine (`UMB-HEPHAESTUS-001`)** existed as separate entities. The "Game" described Prestige and Quests, while the "Engine" described Audits and Weaving. There was no explicit mechanism linking the act of _engineering_ to the reward of _prestige_.

### 1.2 The Spark (The Connection)

We realized that **The Code is the Game**. The Hephaestus Engine is not just a tool; it is the **Dungeon Master** (or Quest Giver) for the RPG Framework. By linking the `Impact Analysis` (Hephaestus) to `Dissonance Quests` (RPG), we create a closed loop where technical debt becomes a playable challenge.

### 1.3 The Resolution (The Bridge)

We forged explicit bidirectional links:

1. **UMB-RPG-MANUAL-001**: Updated to reference `UMB-HEPHAESTUS-001` as the driving engine.
2. **UMB-RPG-001**: Linked to `UMB-PUPT-001` and `UMB-HEPHAESTUS-001`.
3. **UMB-HEPHAESTUS-001**: Linked to `AOP-SENTINEL-SCAN-001` (The Guard) and `GUCA-SIMP-001` (The Oracle).
