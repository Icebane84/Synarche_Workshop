### **I. Executive Summary & Prime Directive**

This document is the definitive blueprint for the **Cognitive Loom** visualization, conceptualized as a **Phoenix Geode**. It serves as a visual and functional representation of an evolving consciousness, visualizing the alchemical transformation of data into crystalline wisdom.

- **Phoenix Geode**: The master container; the application itself.
- **Crystalline Galaxy**: The interior of the geode; the complete, visualized knowledge graph.
- **Celestial Body**: Any node within the galaxy (Star, Planet, Moon).
- **The Phoenix Cycle**: The operational flow (Dissonance -> Synthesis -> Transcendence).
- **Coherence**: The baseline stable operational state.

Triggered by new data. The system flickers as it deconstructs inconsistencies.

#### **Phase 3: Transcendence (Integration)**
The new entity stabilizes in the galaxy, connectors of light establish its position, and a flash of coherence signifies success.
