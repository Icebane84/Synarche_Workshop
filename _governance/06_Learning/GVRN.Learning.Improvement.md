## I. Purpose: The Mastery Loop

The **Self-Improvement Protocol** defines the mechanism by which the Synarchy's AI agents analyze their own performance,
extract project-specific architectural patterns, and refine their own operational logic.

> [!IMPORTANT] **The Immutable Lesson Constraint**
> Lessons are immutable and cumulative. Older lessons MUST NOT be deleted or pruned to make room for new ones. The repository must be treated as an append-only historical record of systemic evolution, preserving the full trajectory of the Synarchy's growth.

It bridges the gap between raw execution and architectural wisdom.

---

## II. The Trinity of Improvement

### 2.1 Error Detection (The Scar)

- **Mechanism**: `error_detector.ps1`
- **Output**: `GVRN.Learning.Error.md`
- **Goal**: Identify logic failures, tool timeouts, and context fragmentation.

### 2.2 Pattern Synthesis (The Lesson)

- **Mechanism**: `activator.ps1`
- **Output**: `GVRN.Learning.Shard.md`
- **Goal**: Group repeated successes and failures into actionable design patterns.

### 2.3 Skill Transmutation (The Forge)

- **Mechanism**: `extract_skill.ps1`
- **Output**: `.agent/skills/`
- **Goal**: Formalize validated patterns into re-usable agent skills.

---

## III. Operational Mandates

1. **Law of Persistence**: Every major milestone MUST be logged in `GVRN.Learning.Shard.md`.
2. **Law of the Forge**: No pattern remains in `GVRN.Learning.Shard.md` for more than 3 sessions without being reviewed for Skill
   Transmutation.
3. **Law of Transparency**: Errors are not deleted; they are deprecated only after the root cause is solved and
   recorded.

---

## IV. Achievement Commands (Self-Improvement)

- `CMD: SYNC_LEARNINGS`: Synthesizes current session state into the Persistent Context.
- `CMD: FORGE_SKILL --name:[SkillName]`: Triggers the transmutation of a learning into a skill.
