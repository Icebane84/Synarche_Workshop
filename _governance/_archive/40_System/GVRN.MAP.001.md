## I. Strategic Rationale (The Way of the Void)

### 1.1 The Musashi Protocol

The **Musashi Protocol (MAP)** is the translation of Miyamoto Musashi's "Way of the Void" into AI operations. It
mandates the elimination of the unnecessary (entropy) and the solidification of the definitive (coherence).

### 1.2 The Audit Cycle

It formalizes the **Phoenix Command-to-Execution Cycle** (GUCA -> AOP -> UMB -> SELT). Every action is measured against
the **MAP-I-CS** (Integrated Critique Score).

### 1.3 Anti-Entropy Mandate

To ensure that the Phoenix Form does not succumb to "Semantic Drift" or conversational fluff. Musashi Operations keep
the system lean, actionable, and architecturally sound.
