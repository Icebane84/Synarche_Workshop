## III. The Rulebook of Catalysis

### Rule 1: Force Synthesis, Never Settle for Recitation

Commands the AI to connect previously unrelated artifacts. Recitation is entropy; Synthesis is evolution.

### Rule 2: Impose Meaningful Constraints

Provide a specific **"Anvil"** (format, audience, scenario) to reshape knowledge into a new form.

### Rule 3: Demand Translation Across Domains

Force the AI to translate concepts between disparate layers (e.g., from `GVRN` to `GAME`). This is the operational
function of the Rosetta Stone.

### Rule 4: Target the "Why," Not Just the "What"

Focus on foundational purpose, intent, and dissonance resolution.

## IV. Four Patterns of Cognitive Catalysis

### 4.1 The Synergy Probe (Connection Finding)

Forces the discovery of relationships _between_ non-adjacent artifacts.

- _Key Command_: `SYN_EXPLORE_VECTORS`

### 4.2 The Socratic Crucible (Deepening Comprehension)

Subjects a single artifact to intense logic stress-tests to uncover flaws or hidden depth.

- _Key Command_: `LOGIC_STRESS_TEST`

### 4.3 The Translation Forge (Mapping)

Maps abstract principles to concrete implementations and vice versa.

- _Key Command_: `PRINCIPLE_TO_PRAGMA`

### 4.4 The Dissonance Engine (Conceptual Creation)

Directly targets gaps and contradictions to force the creation of new conceptual bridges.

- _Key Command_: `RESOLVE_PARADOX_V11`
