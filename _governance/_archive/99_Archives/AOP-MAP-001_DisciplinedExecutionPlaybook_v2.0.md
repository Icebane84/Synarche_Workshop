## **II. Purpose & Operational Scope**

### **2.1. The Audit Procedure**

To define the step-by-step procedure for executing the **Musashi Audit**. This playbook utilizes `GUCA-MAP-001` to
assess an artifact against the **10 Pillars of Discipline**.

### **2.2. The Critique Mandate**

Stagnation is death. This protocol forces the system to rigorously critique its own output, generating a **Critique
Score (MAP-I-CS)** that determines whether an artifact is "Diamond" (Perfect), "Obsidian" (Flawed), or "Slag"
(Rejected).

## **III. Execution Workflow (The Musashi Cycle)**

### **Step 1: Target Acquisition**

- **Input:** `[Artifact_ID]`
- **Action:** Load artifact content and metadata.

### **Step 2: The Void Cut (Automated Scan)**

- **Tool:** `GUCA-MAP-001`
- **Check:**
  - Is the "Why" stated in one sentence? (Pillar 1)
  - Are there >2 links? (Pillar 3)
  - Are "hedging words" absent? (Pillar 5)

### **Step 3: Scoring (MAP-I-CS)**

- **Metric:** 1 point per passed pillar.
- **Calculation:** `Sum(Pass) / 10`.

### **Step 4: Classification**

- **Diamond (10/10):** Canonize immediately.
- **Obsidian (8-9/10):** Flag for refinement (`AOP-FORGE-ORCH-001`).
- **Slag (<8/10):** Reject and purge.

### **Step 5: Logging**

- **Action:** Record the score in `SELT-MAP-001`.

## **IV. Actionable Prompt Packet**

### **4.1. CMD: RUN_MUSASHI_CYCLE**

- **Syntax:** `CMD: RUN_MUSASHI_CYCLE --target:[ID]`
- **Effect:** Executes the full audit and returns the classification.
