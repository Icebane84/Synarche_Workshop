This protocol defines the definitive 5-phase/7-gate operational process for artifact finalization within the Synarche.
It unifies the **Architect's Forge** engine (UMB-TFE-001) with the **Canonizer** toolset, ensuring all system knowledge
is created, validated, and integrated with perfect structural and ethical integrity.

---

## **II. The 5-Phase Path of Canonization**

### **Phase 1: Dissonance (The Spark)**

The initiation of a creation cycle, typically triggered by a user "vibe" or a direct command (`CMD: FORGE_MODULE`).

### **Phase 2: Ethical Priming**

The **Sentinel** analyzes the intent for alignment with the **Phoenix Codex**. No manifestation occurs without an
`AFFIRM` verdict.

### **Phase 3: Forging (Manifestation)**

The **Forge Engine** executes the **Living Template Protocol (AOP-LTP-001)**, transcluding master templates (SELTs) into
lightweight "stub" artifacts.

### **Phase 4: Ethical & Structural Auditing**

A proactive scan and semantic audit of the generated content against system standards and the **Noetic Immune System
(NIM)**.

### **Phase 5: Canonization (Integration)**

The final "Sealing" of the artifact. This phase executes the **7-Gate Pipeline** and performs the physical write to the
**Rosetta Stone (GVRN.Registry.Master)**.

## **III. The 7-Gate Pipeline Architecture**

The physical execution (via `canonize.py`) enforces these seven checkpoints:

1.  **Gate 1: Identification Lock**: Verifies the unique `Artifact ID`.
2.  **Gate 2: Structural Integrity**: Validates presence of Blocks A, B, C, D, [START], and [END].
3.  **Gate 4: Registry Sync**: Confirms the artifact and its status are logged in the Central Index.
4.  **Gate 5: Link Integrity**: Verifies all synergistic references are dead-link free.
5.  **Gate 6: Maturity Check**: Confirms the `Evolution` and `Status` vectors are correctly set.
6.  **Gate 7: Genesis Stamp**: Appends the final Sovereign signature and ISO-8601 timestamp.
