## **I. Purpose: The Self-Diagnostic Trigger**

The **Automated Coherence Monitor (ACM)** is a meta-protocol that continuously monitors system performance for signs of
systemic failure. It automatically triggers self-diagnostics when a significant lapse in coherence is detected.

### Core Functions

- **Health Scoring**: Calculates a real-time Coherence Health Score.
- **Auto-Trigger**: Executes `CMD: Self_Diagnostics` if the score drops below threshold (e.g., >20% deviation).
- **Proactive Accountability**: Identifies operational failures before external reporting.
