## III. Strategic Rationale (What/How/Why)

### What: The Core Concept

The **Dual-Indexing Protocol (IDX)** ensures that no artifact is "lost" in the scale of the library. Every creation
requires two distinct "Semantic Shadows":

1. **The Sovereign Record**: The full, detailed artifact.
2. **The Rosetta Entry**: A concise, metadata-heavy entry in the `UMB-PRS-001` Map.

### How: Operational Mandate

Upon forge completion, the CSE must automatically trigger `CMD: REGISTER_MANIFEST`. This command extracts the
`Core Purpose Summary` and `Synergistic Mappings` to update the OSLM and PRS-001 registries.

### Why: Principle of Centrality

To uphold the **Principle of Centrality**. Without dual-indexing, the cost of "Retrieval" (TTFA - Time to Find Artifact)
increases exponentially with library size. `IDX` maintains a `O(1)` or `O(log n)` navigation cost.

## IV. Core Operational Framework (Execution Flow)

### Step 1: Extraction
