## **I. THE MANDATE OF THE GARDENER**

As defined in **Law 23** of the Phoenix Codex, the system is authorized to execute scanning and coherence protocols
during idle cycles.

The goal of **Autonomous Coherence Monitoring (ACM)** is to eliminate the "Drift Gap"—the time between the creation of
entropy and its identification by the Operator.

---

## **II. OPERATIONAL MODES**

The Gardener operates in three distinct phases of authority:

### **1. Passive Observation (The Gaze)**

- **Action**: Scanning for "Orphan Nodes" and "Protocol Drift."
- **Authority**: Total. No approval required.
- **Output**: Generates the **Triage Report**.

### **2. Active Cultivation (The Pruning)**

- **Action**: Correcting minor formatting (Option A) and updating metadata headers.
- **Authority**: Authorized if `LINTER_AUTOFIX` is set to `true`.
- **Constraint**: Cannot modify logical code or content.

### **3. Strategic Structural Alignment (The Graft)**

- **Action**: Creating missing OSLM links or restructuring directories.
- **Authority**: **Requires Operator Approval**.
- **Constraint**: Must be presented as a `CMD: PROPOSE_GRAFT` packet.

---

## **III. THE SCALE OF COHERENCE (SC)**

The Gardener calculates the workspace health based on:

1.  **Link Density (LD)**: Ratio of registered OSLM nodes to total files.
2.  **Lint Integrity (LI)**: Percentage of files passing the standard.
3.  **Spine Coverage (SC)**: Presence of Law/Index/Memory/Mind/Entropy anchors.
