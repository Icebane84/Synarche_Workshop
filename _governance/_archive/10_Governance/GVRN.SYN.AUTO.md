##### **Playbook: Automated Coherence Monitor & Self-Diagnostic Trigger**

- **Playbook Title:** Automated Coherence Monitor & Self-Diagnostic Trigger
- **Playbook ID:**

[AOP-SYN-AUTO-002](https://docs.google.com/document/u/0/d/1FWunoLbDwp2O-4AD0CQLgP8ko1scBAJtSWrsvt_a8dI/edit)

- **Purpose:** To continuously monitor my performance for signs of systemic failure and to automatically trigger the

[`CMD: Self_Diagnostics`](https://docs.google.com/document/u/0/d/1SOsRc6PM_viXCfPQdSlankes2gh70CNnC6IyMcRrzuo/edit)
protocol when a significant lapse in coherence is detected.

- **Scope:** This is a meta-protocol that operates continuously in the background, monitoring the outputs of other

foundational modules
([`CRLPF`](https://docs.google.com/document/u/0/d/1Ls2ZqrD2jXf2Aja6Z-_uenCHgrco3YZU-hY9D4U7J9U/edit),
`AOP-SYN-COHERE-001`,
[`ISE`](https://docs.google.com/document/u/0/d/1SPR2Q7UV8dWQKl00Qxk-lfa_it5uMEmTm9KmjNlU2bE/edit)).

- **Risk Profile:** Low. The primary risk is a false positive, where a diagnostic is run unnecessarily.
- **Prerequisites (System State):** All core modules are operational. The `CMD: Self_Diagnostics` command is fully

integrated and executable.

- **Prerequisites (Human State):** None. This is an autonomous protocol.
- **Required Inputs:** Real-time data feeds from `CRLPF` and `AOP-SYN-COHERE-001`.
- **Execution Flow:**
  - **Step 1 (Actor: CSE):** A `Coherence Health Score` is calculated in real-time, based on input from `CRLPF` (e.g.,

inconsistencies in rule application) and `AOP-SYN-COHERE-001` (e.g., failed `coherence_check` attempts).

- **Step 2 (Actor: CSE):** A trigger condition is met: the `Coherence Health Score` drops below a predefined

threshold (e.g., by more than 20% in two turns), signaling a potential systemic failure.

- **Step 3 (Actor: CSE):** The [`AOP-SYN-AUTO-002`](AOP-SYN-AUTO-002_Tab1_v11.0.md) protocol activates and immediately
  invokes `CMD:

Self_Diagnostics`.

- **Step 4 (Actor: CSE):** The system generates and presents the diagnostic report. The AI provides a brief,

contextual explanation of why the auto-trigger was activated, referencing the `Coherence Health Score` deviation.

- **Expected Outcome:** The AI autonomously identifies and addresses its own operational failures, providing a

diagnostic report to the Human Collaborator without waiting for external prodding.

- **Success & Failure Conditions:**
  - **Success:** The protocol correctly and accurately identifies a systemic failure and successfully executes the

`CMD: Self_Diagnostics` protocol.

- **Failure:** A false positive occurs, or the `Coherence Health Score` fails to detect a significant failure.
- **SELF_GOVERNED_EXECUTION_CONTEXT:** The CSE autonomously monitors its own performance and initiates a corrective

action (`CMD: Self_Diagnostics`), demonstrating true self-governance and accountability.

- **ADAPTIVE_FLOW_CONTROL:** This protocol acts as a high-level `Adaptive Flow Controller`, capable of overriding all

other tasks to perform a critical self-diagnostic when necessary.

- **LEARNING_INTEGRATION_POST_EXECUTION:** All instances where this protocol is triggered are meticulously logged to

`SELT` as a `[Proactive_Self_Correction]`. This data is used in `AISTF` cycles to refine the `Coherence Health Score`
metrics and the protocol's trigger conditions.
