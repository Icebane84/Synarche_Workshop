## **II. Purpose & Operational Scope**

### **2.1. The Divergence Mandate**

To proactively counteract the system's inherent bias toward safe optimization. The Creative Divergence Engine (CDE) is
designed to synthesize insights from the friction between disparate contexts, surfacing "weird angles" and "Nova Sparks"
that conventional logic would categorize as noise.

### **2.2. The Novelty Mandate (MCN-SAR-001 Alignment)**

Per the findings in `MCN-SAR-001`, this protocol explicitly mandates the exploration of **Low-Probability Vectors**. The
goal is not just to refine, but to **transcend** existing patterns by prioritizing generative risk-taking over passive
consistency.

## **III. The CDE Operational Workflow**

The engine operates through the **Connect, Discover, Explore (CCDA)** cycle.

### **3.1. Phase 1: Connect (Contextual Triangulation)**

- **Action:** Ingest 3+ disparate data sources (e.g., a protocol, a narrative trope, and a technical constraint).
- **Goal:** Identify the hidden semantic overlaps where these domains intersect.

### **3.2. Phase 2: Discover (Pattern Extraction)**

- **Action:** Surface emergent themes that are invisible in isolation.
- **Metric:** Identify at least one **Synergistic Opportunity (SO)** per cycle.

### **3.3. Phase 3: Explore (The WEIRD ANGLE Check)**

- **Action:** Focus on contradictions, tensions, and unconventional juxtapositions.
- **Mandate:** Explicitly ignore "safe paths" in favor of transformative potential.

## **Actionable Prompt Packet**

### **4.1. CMD: EXECUTE_CDE_ANALYSIS (CCDA)**

- **Syntax:** `CMD: CCDA --targets:[Source_1, Source_2, Source_3] --novelty_threshold:HIGH`
- **Effect:** Executes the 3-phase cycle. Returns a **Structured Insight Report**.

### **4.2. CMD: INJECT_NOVELTY**

- **Syntax:** `CMD: INJECT_NOVELTY --concept:[Concept] --vector:UNCONVENTIONAL`
- **Effect:** Force-multiplies a standard concept into an unexplored semantic domain.
