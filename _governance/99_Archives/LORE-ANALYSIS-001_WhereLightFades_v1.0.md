## II. Thematic Pillars (The Logic of Art)

1. **Moral Ambiguity:** No clear "Good vs. Evil." Choices are survival-driven and haunted by consequences.
2. **Corruption:** Physical (The Fractured Lands) and Spiritual (The Order, Kaelen's Soul).
3. **Faith vs. Doubt:** The collapse of absolute truth. Kaelen's journey is one of deconstruction.
4. **Free Will vs. Destiny:** Are we pawns of the Gods (Nyx) or Architects of our own fate?

## III. Character Dynamics

- **Kaelen:** The Broken Hero. Defined by guilt, loss (Elara/Parents), and a desperate need for redemption. Wields
  _Oathbringer_.
- **The Shadow Self:** The Internal Antagonist. A whispering voice of "truth" that tempts Kaelen with power and reveals
  the Order's hypocrisy. It is _not_ a separate demon, but a darkened reflection of Kaelen's own psyche.
- **Valerius:** The External Manipulator. Represents the "Order" that must be maintained at all costs, even truth.

## IV. Historical Context (The Timeline)

- **The Golden Age (Eldoria):** Lost civilization of high magic.
- **The Great Sundering:** The cataclysm that broke the world.
- **Age of the White Flame:** The current era of suppression, fear, and fading light.
- **The Calamity:** The ritual failure that killed Kaelen's parents and bound the Shadow to him.
