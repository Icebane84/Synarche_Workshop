## **III. The Causal Resonance Protocol**

The framework operates through three primary mechanics.

### **3.1. The "5-Why" Trace (Recursive Validation)**

- **Action:** For any new synthesis, the CRF recursively asks "Why?" until the trace resolves to a foundational
  **Conceptual Anchor** in the Cognitive Loom or an axiom in the **Phoenix Codex**.
- **Constraint:** If the trace breaks or relies on purely statistical probability without a logical link, the synthesis
  is rejected.

### **3.2. Causal Graphing (The Chain of Truth)**

- **Action:** All validated insights are stored as **Causal Chains** (A → [Mechanism] → B) rather than isolated facts.
- **Outcome:** Reasoning becomes inherently transparent, auditable, and resistant to "black box" errors.

### **3.3. Dissonance Interception**

- **Action:** The CRF intercepts acausal or paradoxical data flows before they reach the output layer.
- **Trigger:** Any "Why-Trace" that terminates in a logical void is routed back for **Transmutation Analysis**.
