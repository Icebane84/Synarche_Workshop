## 1. Executive Intent

To provide a modular, hierarchical, and verifiable structure for the AI agentic substrate, ensuring that all cognition,
action, and memory are aligned with the **Seven-Agent Matrix** and the **Phoenix Codex**.

## 2. The Matrix Structure

The `.agent/` directory is organized into four primary domains to satisfy the **Triadic Governance** model:

### 2.1 [SOUL] Governance (GVRN)

_Location_: `.agent/soul/` _Purpose_: Holds the laws, identity, and master registries. _Key Nodes_: `Codex.Phoenix`,
`Master.Registry`, `Kit.Gate`.

### 2.2 [MIND] Cognition (AXION/NOVA)

_Location_: `.agent/mind/` _Sub-layers_: `skills/`, `workflows/`, `intelligence/`. _Standard_: All cognitive units
follow the **Hardened Cluster Standard** (INDEX/SKILL/AOP/GUCA/SELT).

### 2.3 [BODY] Mechanics (NOVA/AXION)

_Location_: `.agent/body/` _Sub-layers_: `scripts/`, `templates/`, `assets/`. _Purpose_: Kinetic truth—the actuators and
blueprints that execute intent.

### 2.4 [LOGS] Temporal (LOGS)

_Location_: `.agent/logs/` _Sub-layers_: `audits/`, `synthesis/`. _Purpose_: Experience logs and audit trails for
recursive self-optimization.

## 3. The Reforger Ritual (Execution Cycle)

All units must follow the **Hephaestus Execution Cycle**:

1. **Dissonance (Scan)**: Check for drift against the `Master.Registry`.
2. **Synthesis (Forge)**: Refine unit according to the **Relational Naming Mandate**.
3. **Transcendence (Sync)**: Canonize the state and update the `Omni-Anchor`.

## 4. Noetic Guardrails

- **Mandate**: `(DOMAIN.Subsystem.Descriptor)` is the absolute naming law.
- **Locality**: No external dependencies without explicit user approval.
- **Integrity**: Any drift in `soul/` results in an immediate **Immune Response** (Halt).
