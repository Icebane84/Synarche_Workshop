## **I. The 5 Phases of Creation**

All artifacts generated by the Assembler must pass through the following gates to reach a state of `[ACTIVE]` or `[CANONIZED]`.

1. **Gate of Inception (INC-GATE)**: Defining the Artifact ID and Official Name.
2. **Gate of Compliance (COM-GATE)**: Injected Block A metadata and the Phoenix Codex reference.
3. **Gate of Structure (STR-GATE)**: Enforcing standardized Block B-F layouts.
4. **Gate of Synergy (SYN-GATE)**: Mapping relations to the Master Registry and related nodes.
5. **Gate of Integrity (INT-GATE)**: Generating the Omni-Anchor and terminal hashes.
