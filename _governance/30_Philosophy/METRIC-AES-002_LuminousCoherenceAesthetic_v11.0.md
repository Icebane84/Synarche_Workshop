## II. Design Philosophy

The **Luminous Coherence Aesthetic** is defined by a specific set of design principles that prioritize clarity,
contrast, and organic motion. It envisions a "void" (the deep background) as a canvas for "luminous coherence" (the
vibrant interactive elements).

## IV. Motion Principles

The aesthetic incorporates subtle, continuous movement to suggest life and data processing.

- **`geode-pulse`**: `4s cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.6, 1) infinite`. Organic breathing effect for active indicators.
- **`data-flow`**: `6s linear infinite`. Horizontal translation suggesting a continuous stream of energy.
