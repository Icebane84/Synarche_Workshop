# Podcasts & Audio Deep Dives

Open Notebook can convert your text-based research into engaging audio conversations, similar to "NotebookLM."

## I. How It Works

1.  **Script Generation**: The AI analyzes your sources and writes a script for a conversation between two hosts (e.g., "Sascha" and "Marina").
2.  **Audio Synthesis**: The script is sent to a Text-to-Speech (TTS) engine to generate the audio files.
3.  **Mixing**: The audio clips are stitched together into a single MP3 file.

## II. Configuration

### [2.1] TTS Providers

- **OpenAI Audio**: High quality, paid (HD models recommended).
- **ElevenLabs**: Premium quality, extensive voice cloning.
- **Edge TTS**: Free, decent quality (uses Microsoft Edge's built-in voices).

### [2.2] Generating an Episode

1.  Select your sources.
2.  Click the **Podcast** tab.
3.  **Topic Focus**: (Optional) Tell the hosts what to focus on (e.g., "Focus on the financial implications").
4.  Click **Generate Episode**.

- _Warning: This process can take 2-5 minutes depending on length._

## III. Exporting

Once generated, the podcast appears in your **Media Library**. You can download the MP3 or play it directly in the browser.
