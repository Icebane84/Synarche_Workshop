# Introduction to Open Notebook

**Open Notebook** is an intelligent research assistant designed to help you organize, analyze, and synthesize information from various sources on your local machine.

## I. Core Concept

Think of Open Notebook as a private, AI-powered workspace where you can:

- **Aggregate Sources**: Bring together PDFs, text files, YouTube videos, and web articles.
- **Analyze Context**: Use advanced AI models to read and understand your collected materials.
- **Generate Insights**: Ask questions, summarize complex topics, and generate structured notes.
- **Produce Content**: Turn your research into podcasts, reports, or study guides.

## II. Strategic Advantages

### [2.1] 100% Local & Private

Your data never leaves your machine unless you explicitly choose an external AI provider. All processing, indexing, and storage happen locally.

### [2.2] Flexible Intelligence

Switch between different AI models (OpenAI, Anthropic, Ollama, etc.) to balance cost, speed, and privacy.

### [2.3] Source Grounding

Every answer and note generated is grounded in your specific sources, complete with citations, reducing hallucinations and increasing trust.

## III. Key Features Overview

- **Notebook organization**: Group related research into dedicated notebooks.
- **Multi-modal sources**: Support for text, PDF, and audio/video content.
- **Chat with Context**: Ask questions specifically about the documents in your notebook.
- **AI Note Generation**: Create structured notes from your raw sources.
- **Audio Podcasts**: Generate "Deep Dive" style audio summaries of your content.
